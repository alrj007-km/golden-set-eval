## 03 · Installation

Installation typically takes 20–30 minutes. Follow each step in sequence and double-check all connections before restoring power.

1. **Remove Your Existing Thermostat**
   - Turn off HVAC power at the circuit breaker.
   - Carefully remove the faceplate of your current thermostat.
   - Photograph the wiring configuration — label each wire if needed.
   - Unscrew or unclip the wires and remove the old wall plate.

2. **Mount the AURRA Wall Plate**
   - Position the AURRA wall plate over the existing wall opening.
   - Use a pencil to mark the four mounting holes.
   - Secure the plate with the included screws, using the built-in bubble level to ensure it's perfectly straight.
   - Pass wires through the center opening.

3. **Connect the Wires**

   Match each wire to its labeled terminal using the reference table below. Press the orange connector button, insert the wire, then release to lock.

   | Terminal | Wire Color | Function |
   |---|---|---|
   | R | Red | Power (24V AC) |
   | C | Blue or Black | Common (Return) |
   | W / W1 | White | Heat Stage 1 |
   | W2 | White / Brown | Heat Stage 2 |
   | Y / Y1 | Yellow | Cooling Stage 1 |
   | G | Green | Fan |
   | O / B | Orange / Blue | Heat Pump Reversing Valve |

4. **Attach the Thermostat and Restore Power**
   - Align the AURRA display module with the wall plate — it snaps into place with a single click.
   - Return to the circuit breaker and restore power to your HVAC system.
   - The AURRA display will illuminate, indicating it's ready for app activation.

> **Note:** If the display does not illuminate after restoring power, verify that all wire connections are fully seated and that the circuit breaker is in the ON position.

## 04 · App Activation

With AURRA installed and powered on, complete setup using the AURRA app. The full process takes under five minutes.

1. **Download the AURRA App**
   - Scan the QR code on the back of the AURRA unit, or search **AURRA Home** in the App Store or Google Play.
   - Create your account using your email address.
   - You'll receive a verification email — confirm it before proceeding.

2. **Connect to Wi-Fi**
   - Open the AURRA app and tap **Add New Device**.
   - Follow the in-app prompts to connect AURRA to your home Wi-Fi (2.4GHz or 5GHz).
   - Once connected, your device will appear as **My AURRA Thermostat**.

3. **Personalize Your Home Profile**
   - Enter your home's location for weather-aware temperature adjustments.
   - Select your HVAC system type from the dropdown list.
   - Set your preferred heating and cooling temperature ranges.
   - Tap **Save** — your AI climate journey begins.

> **Tip:** Enable location access for the AURRA app to unlock Geofencing (Home/Away automation). AURRA will automatically adjust temperatures when you leave or return home.

## 05 · Smart Feature: Scheduling

Scheduling lets AURRA automatically maintain the right temperature throughout your day — without any manual adjustments. Set it once and let the system handle the rest.

1. **Open the Schedule Section**
   - Open the AURRA app and tap the menu icon ☰ in the top-right corner.
   - Navigate to **Schedule** and tap **+ New Schedule**.
   - Give your schedule a name (e.g., *Workday Routine* or *Weekend Mode*).

2. **Define Your Time Blocks**
   - Tap **+ Add Time Block** and set a start time, end time, and target temperature.
   - Create separate blocks for Morning, Day, Evening, and Night.
   - Select which days of the week each block applies to, then tap **Save**.

### Suggested Default Schedule

| Period | Time Range | Heat | Cool | Use Case |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Morning | 4:00 AM – 8:00 AM | 70°F | 74°F | Wake up comfortably |
| Day | 8:00 AM – 4:30 PM | 65°F | 78°F | Energy saving while away |
| Evening | 4:30 PM – 10:00 PM | 72°F | 73°F | Relax after work |
| Night | 10:00 PM – 4:00 AM | 68°F | 76°F | Sleep comfortably |

> **Tip:** Enable AI Auto-Adjust in Schedule settings. AURRA will refine your schedule over 7 days using occupancy sensors and your manual override history.

## 06 · Smart Feature: Energy-Saving Mode

AURRA's Eco Mode minimizes energy consumption without sacrificing comfort. By combining smart temperature limits, occupancy detection, and utility-rate awareness, AURRA can reduce HVAC energy use by up to 23% annually.

- **Lower Bills** — Reduce usage during peak hours or when you're away from home.
- **Eco-Friendly** — Minimize your carbon footprint with smart temperature adjustments.
- **Customizable** — Full control over energy savings limits and threshold preferences.

1. **Enable Eco Mode**
   - Open the AURRA app → ☰ Menu → **Climate Control**.
   - Toggle **Eco Mode** to ON.
   - Set your Eco temperature limits: Heating: 62°F · Cooling: 78°F.

2. **Configure Energy-Saving Features**

   | Feature | What It Does |
   |---|---|
   | Auto Away | Detects when no motion is present and adjusts to Eco temperatures automatically. |
   | Peak Hour Savings | Reduces HVAC load during high-demand grid hours (utility provider support required). |
   | Monthly Reports | Tracks energy usage, savings achieved, and environmental impact over time. |

3. **Monitor and Adjust**
   - Review your energy usage data in the **Energy Reports** section of the app.
   - Adjust Eco temperature limits seasonally for maximum savings.
   - Enable **Peak Hour Notifications** to receive alerts when significant savings are being captured.

> **Eco Tip:** Pairing AURRA's Eco Mode with utility rate scheduling can reduce your HVAC energy cost by an estimated $180–$340 per year, depending on your home size and location.

## 07 · Smart Feature: Voice Control

Control your home climate hands-free using AURRA's integrations with the three major smart home voice platforms. Each setup takes under two minutes.

### Amazon Alexa

1. Open the **Amazon Alexa** app on your smartphone.
2. Tap **More** → **Skills & Games** and search for **AURRA**.
3. Tap **Enable to Use** and sign in with your AURRA account credentials.
4. Say *"Alexa, discover my devices"* — your thermostat will appear in the Devices list.

### Google Assistant

1. Open the **Google Home** app and tap **Add (+)** → **Set up device**.
2. Select **Works with Google** and search for **AURRA Smart Thermostat**.
3. Sign in with your AURRA account and grant the requested permissions.
4. Say *"Hey Google, sync my devices"* to complete discovery.

### Apple HomeKit

1. Open the **Home** app on your iPhone or iPad.
2. Tap **Add Accessory** and scan the HomeKit setup code on the AURRA unit.
3. Follow the on-screen prompts to assign AURRA to a room and complete setup.
4. Your thermostat will appear automatically in the Home app — no discovery command needed.

### Voice Command Reference

| Platform | Example Command | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Any | "Set [room] to 72 degrees." | Set target temperature |
| Any | "Turn on Eco Mode." | Enable Eco Mode |
| Any | "What's the humidity?" | Read current humidity |
| Alexa | "Alexa, set the living room to cool." | Switch to cooling mode |
| Google | "Hey Google, what's the thermostat set to?" | Read current setpoint |
| HomeKit | "Hey Siri, set the thermostat to 70 degrees." | Set temperature via Siri |

## 08 · Troubleshooting

The following covers the most common setup and operation questions. For issues not listed here, visit aurrahome.com/support or contact our team directly.

### FAQs & Quick Fixes

**My schedule isn't saving.**
Confirm AURRA is connected to Wi-Fi and that Schedule is toggled ON in the app. If the issue persists, force-close and reopen the AURRA app, then try again.

**Temperatures are changing unexpectedly.**
Check for overlapping time blocks or conflicting schedule entries. Note that AI Auto-Adjust is active during the first 7 days of use — minor deviations during this learning period are normal and will stabilize.

**My voice assistant isn't responding to AURRA commands.**
Confirm the AURRA skill or integration is still enabled in your voice assistant's app. Unlink and relink the AURRA account if commands remain unresponsive. Ensure your voice assistant firmware is up to date.

**The AURRA app says "Device Not Found."**
Verify that AURRA is powered on and that your smartphone is on the same Wi-Fi network. Tap Menu → Device Settings → Rediscover Device. If the issue continues, perform a soft reset by holding the AURRA display button for 10 seconds.

**My voice commands are being misunderstood.**
Update the AURRA app to the latest version. Use clear, complete phrasing — say "Set the thermostat to 70 degrees" rather than "Set it warmer."

**AURRA isn't turning on after installation.**
Confirm the circuit breaker is in the ON position and all wires are fully seated in their terminals. If a C-wire is absent, install the included C-wire adapter and retry.
